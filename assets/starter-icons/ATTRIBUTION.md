# Starter Icon Pack Attribution

## Summary

The POE2GPS v0.36 starter icon pack is 20 icons rasterized from source SVGs at [game-icons.net](https://game-icons.net/), an open-source game-asset collection. All icons in this pack are licensed under [CC BY 3.0](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/3.0/). Attribution follows the CC BY 3.0 requirement: the source, author, license, and any modifications are recorded per icon below.

Contributors represented in this pack:
- **Lorc** — https://lorcblog.blogspot.com/
- **Delapouite** — https://delapouite.com/
