## Known risks + mitigations (from audit)

1. **B2 (Entity chain root) is complex + touches the render hot path.** LOCKED-DECISIONS block in the bead spec explicitly names the two struct bases (EntityDetails vs. reached-via-ComponentLookUpPtr) â€” per `feedback_workflow_decomposer_drift` memory.

2. **B5 (Flags toggle-diff) requires human-in-loop workflow.** Bead ships diagnostic-only; auto-heal from identified offset deferred to v0.42.x follow-up.

3. **`healed-offsets.json` persistence risk on subsequent patches shifting BACK.** Mitigation baked into B0 (30-day invalidation + signature re-check on cold start).

4. **Diagnostic endpoint proliferation (9 new).** Mitigation: all under `/api/probe/*` prefix so future consolidation into a single `/api/probe` unified endpoint is easy.

5. **Ripple risk on `OffsetHealthMonitor`.** Each bead's hook is an independent HealthState channel (not shared).

6. **Worker success rate on complex UI beads (B3/B5/B6) empirically ~60% first-try** â€” frontier-fix-in-place is the mitigation, do NOT re-dispatch unchanged specs. Budget 2-3 salvage passes across the arc.
